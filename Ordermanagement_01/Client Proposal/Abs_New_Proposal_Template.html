TITLE SERVICES

The title search process involves examining all documents recorded on public record that are filed against owners or a legal description of the desired property.

We prepare all kinds of reports suitable for the Title Needs. Commitment Reports, Current Owner, Property Reports, Two Owner, Updates, O&E Reports, Preliminary Reports, Foreclosure Reports.
